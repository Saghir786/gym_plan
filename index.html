Beginner Gym Routine

3 days per week · 45–60 minutes · 3 sets of 8–12 reps · 60–90s rest
PWA — Installable
Day 1 — Push Day 2 — Pull Day 3 — Legs + Core

How to Use This

  • Pick any 3 non‑consecutive days per week (e.g., Mon · Wed · Fri).
  • Use a weight that gets you close to 12 clean reps; when you hit 12 on all 3 sets, increase weight next time.
  • Move slowly, control the negative, stop 1 rep before failure.
Warm‑up 5 minutes, then do 1–2 lighter sets before your working sets.
